## Description

I am SomaXBT, an independent on-chain investigator specializing in blockchain forensics, crypto threat intelligence, and illicit fund tracing.

I have been actively conducting investigations since 2023, and my work is fully supported by community donations and grants.

My work began after personally experiencing a social engineering attack that resulted in the loss of my own crypto assets. That experience drove me to understand attacker behavior and help others avoid similar outcomes.

Since then, I have evolved into a full-time investigator focused on tracking stolen funds, analyzing attacker methodologies, and uncovering laundering patterns across chains

I contribute as an intelligence contributor to the [SEAL Intel](https://securityalliance.org/our-work/seal-intel) Team, where I support investigations and share actionable intelligence on scams, exploits, and illicit fund movements.

Alongside investigative work, I actively contribute to education in blockchain forensics by publishing structured research and guides to help others understand real-world crypto investigations. My educational articles include:

  * [The Crypto Threat Landscape: Threats and Exploits Targeting Crypto Users ](https://paragraph.com/@somaxbt/the-crypto-threat-landscape-threats-and-exploits-targeting-crypto-users-2)
  * [Blockchain Forensics: A Practical Guide to Tracing Stolen Funds](https://paragraph.com/@somaxbt/blockchain-forensics-a-practical-guide-to-tracing-stolen-funds)
  * [ Blockchain Forensics: Attribution Techniques and the Role of OSINT](https://paragraph.com/@somaxbt/blockchain-forensics-attribution-techniques-and-the-role-of-osint)
  * [Blockchain Forensics: Advanced Blockchain Forensics Techniques and Additional Resources](https://paragraph.com/@somaxbt/blockchain-forensics-advanced-blockchain-forensics-techniques-and-additional-resources)
