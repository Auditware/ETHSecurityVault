## Description

# Project Overview

Every change to Ethereum begins as an [EIP](https://eipsinsight.com/resources/faq), but the process that takes it from idea to implementation has long relied on fragmented tools, manual coordination, and limited visibility. EIPsInsight was built to fix this, bringing structure, transparency, and data-driven coordination to Ethereum’s governance process. 

Today, [EIPsInsight](https://eipsinsight.com/home) is already embedded in EIP’s core workflows. Its [PR Board](https://eipsinsight.com/tools/board?status=Waiting+on+Editor&page=1) is used weekly by EIP Editors to manage proposals and generate agendas, while its [analytics page](https://eipsinsight.com/analytics/prs) has informed editor selection and surfaced critical issues in open PRs. The project was also selected as a grant recipient of [**_Ethereum Foundation ESP grant Q2 2025_**](https://blog.ethereum.org/2025/07/23/allocation-q2-25)** __** , with deliverables 

  * real-time [upgrade tracking](https://eipsinsight.com/upgrade), 
  * EIPs [monthly insights](https://eipsinsight.com/insights/year-month-analysis), 
  * Proposals lifecycle [analytics](https://eipsinsight.com/analytics/eips), 
  * contributor [education resources](https://eipsinsight.com/resources), 
  * the [EIP Creator Tool](https://eipsinsight.com/tools/eip-builder#split#eip#new), and 
  * customizable status notifications was completed on time. That track record is not a claim. It is a [commit history](http://github.com/AvarchLLC/eipsinsight).



  


# The Problem Statement

With the introduction of [EIP-4844](https://eipsinsight.com/eips/4844), Ethereum now operates a live blob fee market powering rollups, but without public tooling to monitor cost efficiency, detect congestion, or assess economic risks. This creates blind spots for both governance and infrastructure operators.

# What we are building next

The next chapter of EIPsInsight is **Rollup Cost Insight** (BlobLens) - an open-source extension _for_** _blob economics_** analytics that closes the two largest gaps in Ethereum's L2 observability layer - _visibility into the economics and risks introduced by EIP-4844 and the emerging blob fee market_.

  


**Gap 1: No open per-rollup DA cost-efficiency scoring.** No public tool computes cost per byte actually used, blob fullness ratios, amortized DA cost per L2 transaction, or blob coordination opportunity scores across rollups. Rollup Cost Insight will build this: a self-hostable efficiency scoring engine updated every epoch, a public rollup leaderboard that any researcher or operator can query.

  


**Gap 2: No live blob fee market health layer.** No public system classifies the current market regime, forecasts congestion from excess_blob_gas accumulation, or pushes threshold alerts to rollup operators. Rollup Cost Insight will build this: a real-time regime classifier (undersaturated / healthy / congested / spike), a 4–12 slot congestion forecast, and a webhook alert system for rollup teams.

  


Both modules connect back to EIPsInsight's governance layer. By connecting protocol governance with live economic data, EIPsInsight ensures that Ethereum’s future [BPO upgrades](https://eipsinsight.com/eips/8135) are informed, observable, and accountable. The same public-goods ethos that has driven every line of EIPsInsight since day one.

  


## Why your donation matters

Quadratic funding means breadth of support matters as much as amount. A donation of any size - $5, $10, $50 signals that the Ethereum community values this work and multiplies the matching pool allocation toward it.

  


EIPsInsight does not have a token. It does not have venture backing. It does not have a protocol treasury. It is funded by grants and community support, built in public, and given away free. The PR Board that EIP Editors use every week, the Editor Analytics that inform [governance appointments](https://github.com/ethcatherders/EIPIP/issues/394), all of it runs on this model.

  


Built as a public good, EIPsInsight strengthens both governance security and rollup ecosystem resilience ensuring Ethereum can scale with clarity, coordination, and trust.

  


If you have ever submitted an EIP, relied on an ERC, used a rollup, or cared about Ethereum's governance being done right, this is the infrastructure that supports it. Help us keep it running and help us build what comes next.
