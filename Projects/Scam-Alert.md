## Description

Scam Alert is a crypto-focused intelligence and reporting platform designed to combat the growing threat of fraud across the digital asset ecosystem. As blockchain adoption accelerates, so does the sophistication and scale of scams, ranging from phishing attacks and impersonation schemes to rug pulls and social engineering. Despite the transparency of blockchain, victims often lack accessible tools to report incidents, verify threats, or recover lost funds. Scam Alert addresses this gap by creating a unified, user-driven platform that empowers individuals and organizations to detect, report, and prevent crypto-related fraud in real time.

  


At its core, Scam Alert enables users to submit verified scam reports, flag suspicious wallet addresses, domains, and social media accounts, and access a continuously updated database of known threats. The platform aggregates and analyzes this data to identify patterns, high-risk actors, and emerging scam trends. Through this intelligence layer, users—including retail investors, compliance teams, and investigators—gain actionable insights that improve decision-making and risk mitigation.

  


In addition, Scam Alert aims to integrate with wallets, exchanges, and Web3 applications via APIs, enabling proactive alerts before users interact with malicious entities. This shifts fraud prevention from reactive to preventative. The platform also supports collaboration with law enforcement, compliance professionals, and blockchain analytics providers to enhance investigation workflows and improve asset tracing outcomes.

  


Beyond technology, Scam Alert is driven by a mission to increase transparency and accountability in crypto. By lowering the barrier to reporting scams and making threat intelligence openly accessible, the project contributes to a safer and more trustworthy digital asset ecosystem. Ultimately, Scam Alert is not just a tool—it is a collective defense layer against crypto crime, built for the community and powered by shared intelligence.
