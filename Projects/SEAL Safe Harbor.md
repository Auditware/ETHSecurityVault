## Description

We are SEAL Safe Harbor - and we make it safe for whitehats to do the right thing. We're a SEAL (Security Alliance) Initiative - SEAL is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded by samczsun that serves as neutral security infrastructure for crypto.

  


When an exploit is underway, whitehats often have the skills to step in and save the funds. But without legal protection, they risk getting sued for helping. That fear keeps many from acting. We think that's broken!

  


Safe Harbor fixes it. Protocols adopt our on-chain agreement to pre-authorize responsible whitehat intervention during active exploits. Follow the rules, you're protected. Whitehats earn a bounty defined by the protocol and rescued funds go back within 72 hours.

  


This isn't theoretical. Whitehats operating under Safe Harbor have already saved ~$6.8M in real rescues - $3M during the Makina incident and $3.8M during the Balancer exploit across multiple chains!

  


Over two dozen protocols have adopted Safe Harbor so far, protecting over $62B in TVL - more than 50% of all DeFi TVL. Protocols like Aave, Lido, Uniswap, Pendle, PancakeSwap, Balancer, and zkSync have all signed on because they believe in a safer ecosystem for everyone.

  


Our mission is to make Safe Harbor the industry standard - so that whenever funds are at risk and an exploit is imminent, any whitehat can step in and protect users without hesitation. It's another layer of defense for the entire ecosystem. The more protocols that adopt, the safer everyone is.

  


Your donation goes directly toward SEAL Safe Harbor - supporting the contributors who run this initiative day to day and the tooling that keeps our work going: on-chain agreement maintenance, protocol onboarding, legal framework stewardship, and whitehat coordination during active rescues. The biggest line item is operating costs for the legal, protocol-relations, and engineering contributors doing the work.

  


Note: SEAL Safe Harbor is an initiative of SEAL (Security Alliance), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Donations made through this Giveth project stay with SEAL Safe Harbor and support the contributors and infrastructure dedicated to this initiative - they are not pooled across SEAL's other initiatives.
