## Description

Freezing stolen crypto is hard. Recovering it is even harder. The Coalition to Change Crypto Freezes and Recovery is bringing the industry together, from DeFi and VASPs to lawyers and regulators, to try to better address our response to stolen fund activity without solely relying on law enforcement to mitigate risks.

  


Crypto hacks, especially those from North Korea, highlight the inadequacy of current approaches to freezing and recovering stolen funds: despite good intentions, existing processes are too slow, fragmented, and reliant on law enforcement court orders. If the industry continues in its current state, its ability to support victims will continue to erode, ultimately undermining trust in crypto as a whole.

  


But together we’re finding ways to get stolen funds off of services’ books in a legally backed way that does not require additional internal resources. Some key areas include:

  


  * Engaging with geographically dispersed law enforcement bodies
  * Standardising processes and information, including evidence quality with the Trusted Investigator Recognition
  * Recovering funds via Indemnity or Hold Harmless Agreements, Arbitration, and DeFi fee sequestration
  * Better data sharing with private entities
  * Advocating for better response infrastructure through the private and public sectors



  


Since our start in Fall 2025 we have brought together hundreds of people within the ecosystem. We’ve seen progress in DeFi’s response and Hold Harmless Agreements, but there’s still more work to be done. Support the Coalition to continue to give hope to victims of crypto thefts.
