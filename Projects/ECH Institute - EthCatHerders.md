## Description

# Who We Are

[ECH Institute](https://echinstitute.org/) is a U.S. 501(c)(3) nonprofit and the institutional home of Ethereum's community-led protocol support group. We formally incorporated as a **nonprofit in 2024** , giving a legal entity to support the Ethereum ecosystem.

  


_ECH Institute is uniquely positioned at the intersection of_** _People, Process, and Protocol_**.

  


# What We Do

Every protocol improvement passes through a rigorous, open governance process. We coordinate EIPs, support network upgrades, produce [educational videos and podcasts](https://www.youtube.com/@echinstitute), facilitate [EIP Editing Office Hours](https://github.com/ethereum/pm/issues?q=is%3Aissue%20state%3Aclosed%20EIP%20Editing%20Office%20Hour), and run [**_Women in Ethereum Protocol (WiEP)_** ](https://www.wiep.info/)study group cohorts (in association with the Ethereum Foundation) to onboard technical women into Ethereum protocol development. Our work is credibly neutral and owned by the whole community. As a neutral public good, we bridge gaps by empowering individuals with blockchain knowledge, strengthening communities, and supporting the Web3 ecosystem with a strong focus on **_education and inclusion & diversity_**.

## EIP Process Management

We support the full EIP and ERC lifecycle — from draft review through finalization. Weekly Editing Office Hours, monthly EIPIP governance meetings, and open GitHub stewardship keep proposals moving and the process transparent. We organized [EIP Editors workshop](https://youtube.com/live/rCTx-K-aArM) & facilitated onboarding new EIP editor. Also, organized the **first**[**EIP Summit** at Devconnect 2025](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL4cwHXAawZxo_4-sDIY0ISXklLYn0ESpC) bringing EIP Editors, authors, contributors and implementers in one room.

  


## Network Upgrade Coordination

  


We coordinate and livestreamed most Ethereum network upgrades since 2019. For each hard fork including [Pectra](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL4cwHXAawZxqOHV_F40AJbzcl8b6tG8xw) and [Fusaka](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL4cwHXAawZxoIenfk7OJry4rxcqX-eqBt) (in 2025), ECH produces educational episodes for every included EIP, documents All Core Devs meetings, manages testnet post-mortems, runs community watch parties, and distributes author-recognition NFTs. **12+ upgrades supported; 200+ explainer videos produced.**

  


## PEEPanEIP & The Fusaka Files Podcast

  


[PEEPanEIP](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL4cwHXAawZxqu0PKKyMzG_3BJV_xZTi1F) (135+ episodes) is Ethereum's definitive EIP education series: hour-long conversations with EIP authors covering every major proposal. In 2025 we launched The Fusaka Files - a limited **_enterprise-focused_** series co-hosted with Paul Brody (EY Global Blockchain Leader and EEA Chairman), bridging protocol changes like EIP-7594 (PeerDAS), EIP-7825, and EIP-7917 to enterprise and institutional audiences. Guests have included [Tomasz Stańczak](https://youtu.be/KBYnFfGokuc?si=wOs_Mvy6oQpOerTV), prev. Co-Executive Director of Ethereum, [Tim Beiko](https://youtu.be/9Ajj6Z_rBCo?si=s_qrsrCSXBrb2uk6), [Alex Stokes](https://youtu.be/KBYnFfGokuc?si=i3_WMlPStcEv1BfF), [Justin Drake](https://youtu.be/GR3Txx2z8nU?si=NxVWx42a2KCDCJUk) and other Ethereum Foundation researchers.

  


## Women in Ethereum Protocol (WiEP)

  


Co-founded with the Ethereum Foundation at Edge City Denver on February 29, 2024, [WiEP](https://github.com/wiepteam/studygroup/tree/main) is the only program dedicated to onboarding technical women into Ethereum's core protocol development. In under two years: **4 cohorts completed, 36 women developers onboarded from 21 countries** , with ongoing study groups, mentorship, and open-source contributions. Events held at [EthCC](https://youtu.be/PO7y3bngGAY?si=5M6vFderd6A7PfO9), [EthDenver](https://youtu.be/xXZgc551NRs?si=I94Gw_IkJWzr8R2c), [EthSingapore](https://youtu.be/v9G684uz6Rk?si=gTsJHHIubVLFeAmR) (ArtScience Museum, with Ethereum Foundation President **_Aya Miyaguchi_**), [Devcon](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rksdvA4oHWU), [EDCON](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uVj7JyTbLbg), [Devconnect](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MiYQ_P7vtEQ&list=PL4cwHXAawZxpbEZhQYgOW8mbQDbLoyQDT). WiEP is an amaging opportunity to learn and contribute to the Ethereum ecosystem.

  


## Enterprise Ethereum Education

  


Protocol changes are not optional background noise for organizations building on Ethereum — they directly affect smart contract compatibility, tooling, and infrastructure planning. ECH Institute provides the clearest window into Ethereum's governance process for enterprise teams. Through our **EEA partnership** and co-produced [**The Fusaka Files**](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL4cwHXAawZxpz-erUbKKUnnGoQNdF8s7Z) with Paul Brody, we translate governance proposals, scalability upgrades, and privacy standards into business-context education for teams at EY, JPMorgan, Microsoft, and beyond.

  


## Community Partnership & Onboarding

ECH’s community partnerships focus on onboarding the next generation of contributors into Ethereum’s governance and protocol processes. Through initiatives like [Road to Devcon](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL4cwHXAawZxoHCcxojowDv37_h7TSFOif) and our collaboration with [EthCluj](https://youtu.be/bcFiI684C8A), we create accessible entry points for learning, collaboration, and meaningful participation. Our Learn2Earn platform rewards users for learning Ethereum protocol concepts via quizzes and **free NFTs - deployed across Dencun and Pectra upgrade cycles**. Our open contributor program, bounty system, ACD Internship, and Discord community (20,000+ members) continuously lower the barrier to participation in Ethereum governance for developers, writers, and researchers worldwide.

  


# Why This Matters

ECH Institute is not building a product. **_We are maintaining and expanding the public infrastructure that makes Ethereum's open governance work_**. No single company owns this work; which is exactly why it needs sustained community funding.

  


Our **2026 priorities** reflect where the gaps are largest:

  * **Strengthening EIP** process research & security, alongside protocol coordination and public governance.
  * **Enterprise education** on Ethereum upgrades, protocol changes, and ecosystem projects
  * **EIP Summit** — bringing the global EIP community together
  * **Expanding WiEP** -scaling the only program onboarding technical women into Ethereum protocol
  * **Continued podcast** production & content creation.



  


_ECH Institute makes Ethereum's evolution visible, accessible, and reliable, and through WiEP, ensures the next generation of protocol builders reflects the full breadth of human talent._
