## Description

## **Who we are**

ClaraHacks is a team of blockchain security researchers and engineers working at the frontier of **AI-driven security for decentralized finance (DeFi)**.

We build advanced systems that help analyze, understand, and respond to real-world blockchain attacks — and we are already integrating these systems into **practical security workflows used by professionals today**.

##   


## **What we are building**

ClaraHacks develops **AI-powered infrastructure for automated attack analysis and response**.

Instead of relying on slow, manual investigation, our technology can:

  * 🔍 Reconstruct complex blockchain attacks from minimal data
  * 🧠 Identify root causes and vulnerabilities
  * ⚡ Generate actionable insights in minutes, not hours
  * 🛡️ Support real-time decision-making for security teams



Our work builds on cutting-edge research showing that AI can dramatically accelerate understanding of blockchain exploits and improve response times .

##   


## **Why this matters**

Blockchain is now critical financial infrastructure — but security has not kept up.

  * Billions of dollars have been lost to exploits
  * Attack analysis is still largely manual and slow
  * Delays in understanding attacks can lead to further losses



In practice, even when attacks are visible on-chain, **turning raw data into clear explanations takes too long**  .

ClaraHacks addresses this gap by making **security faster, more scalable, and more reliable**.

##   


## **How we do it**

We combine **AI, program analysis, and real-world integration** :

  * Build intelligent systems that automatically analyze blockchain activity
  * Integrate directly into **existing security and monitoring workflows**
  * **Deliver structured outputs: explanations, insights, and reproducible cases**
  * **Continuously improve through real-world usage and feedback**



Rather than building tools in isolation, we focus on **systems that are actually used in practice**.
