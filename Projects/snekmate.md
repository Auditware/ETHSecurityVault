## Description

While there are plenty of Solidity libraries out there, nobody has been working on high-quality, secure, and easy-to-integrate library-like Vyper code. 🐍 snekmate fundamentally changed this! In one sentence: **🐍 snekmate are state-of-the-art, highly opinionated, hyper-optimised, and secure Vyper smart contract building blocks**. Or in other words: What OpenZeppelin is for Solidity, 🐍 snekmate is for Vyper. 🐍 snekmate has helped numerous Vyper builders (e.g. Curve, Aerodrome Finance). Not only does client diversity matter but also language diversity!
