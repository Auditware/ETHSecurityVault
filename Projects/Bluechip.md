## Description

Stablecoins are the backbone of DeFi, but most users still can't answer a basic question: is my stablecoin actually safe? Terra showed what happens when that question goes unasked. Bluechip exists to make sure it never goes unanswered.

  


Bluechip is the first, independent stablecoin rating agency built as a public good for the Ethereum ecosystem. Our security research benefits stablecoin holders, DAO contributors, and DeFi protocols by allowing them to make informed decisions. All ratings are available via our website (bluechip.org) or API.

  


Our SMIDGE framework is the first publicly available, factor-based risk model for rating stablecoin safety. We score six factors (Stability, Management, Implementation, Decentralization, Governance, and Externals) and assign letter-grade ratings (from A+ to F). These factors cover what actually matters: reserve composition, redemption rights, smart contract risk, and more.

  


We also host an annual conference dedicated to crypto safety that draws a curated audience of institutional users. The Bluechip25 edition was hosted in partnership with the Ethereum Foundation. Your support helps us rate more stablecoins, and develop frameworks to expand beyond stablecoins.
