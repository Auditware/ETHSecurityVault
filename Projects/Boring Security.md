## Description

## **About Us**

Boring Security is a public good founded in 2022. We are a leader in vendor-neutral Web3 security education. We aim to make security accessible through free, interactive classes and gamified learning.

  


Our approach combines live classes, structured modules, and social content. 

  


## **Our Impact**

  * Educated over 3,000 alumni (holding our alumni token)
  * Taught more than 6,000 students through our live classes 
  * 10,000 Discord users and 400 other Discord communities utilize our materials and alerts to stay safe. 
  * Over $3 million in stolen assets have been recovered through Flashbot rescue operations.



  


## **Our Team**

  * Feld is our founder. He has taught DeFi Basics classes at a major NYC hedge fund and is a primary instructor for Boring Security's class offerings. As a DeFi degen, he often leverages more than 10 DeFi protocols monthly and follows the space closely.
  * Redzin is an instructor, community manager, Discord Auditor, and Web3 Security expert who plays a key role in managing the student experience, coordinating and maintaining the student pipeline for all who take and complete the course.
  * Jam is our grant writer and partner manager within the DAO. He will be checking in with the milestones, acquiring partners for classes, and leading the execution of the grant.



  


## **Our Curriculum**

  * Web3 Security 101 - the basics and fundamentals of staying safe in the crypto scene.
  * Web3 Security 102 - an upskilling and more advanced version of Web3 Security 101
  * DeFi Safety and Security - a comprehensive class on all about DeFi, scams, and risks.
  * Solana Security - an SVM version of our security classes, which are mostly geared towards Ethereum.
  * Sleuthing - in partnership with Intelligence Onchain- teaches how to use blockchain analytics and tracking tools.
  * Social Media Security - X, Discord, TG, and other soc med operational security practices.
