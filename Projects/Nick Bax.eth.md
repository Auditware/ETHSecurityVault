## Description

Hey, I'm Nick. I've been doing cryptocurrency security research for about 9 years and specialize in developing complex on-chain analysis methods. I have applied these methods to disrupt a wide range of threat actors, ranging from physical robbers, to pig butchers, DeFi exploiters and many, many North Korean state-sponsored hackers.

  


A significant chunk of my time is spent working with other researchers like [Tanuki42](https://giveth.io/project/tanuki42) working to disrupt DPRK activities. I am also a part of [SEAL 911](https://giveth.io/project/seal-911) and SEAL Intel.

  


Matters I was _publicly_ involved in, in the last year or so, includes:

  * [DEGEN token exploit](https://x.com/degentokenbase/status/1966432357868503530)
  * [Drift Protocol incident response](https://x.com/davijlu/status/2044768228275036220 )
  * [Loopscale exploit](https://x.com/marygooneratne/status/1920485348024455374)
  * [KelpDAO](https://x.com/KelpDAO/status/2046481564616523976)
  * [LastPass data breach thefts](https://krebsonsecurity.com/2023/09/experts-fear-crooks-are-cracking-keys-stolen-in-lastpass-breach/)



I've also been very involved in many, many incident responses that are non-public, which have resulted in large amounts of money either not being stolen, or being returned to their rightful owners.

Public methods and tools for advanced cryptocurrency analysis which I have developed include tools for [Tornado demixing](https://x.com/zachxbt/status/1732790508714430685) and two of the only public examples of Monero tracing. ([1](https://medium.com/@nbax/tracing-the-wannacry-2-0-monero-transactions-d8c1e5129dc1))([2](https://blockworks.co/news/hacker-stole-bitcoin?__cf_chl_rt_tk=o8COzSmnoOSsHOs.tdajSxwQ.YCnWGqpbuCqa6waxoQ-1776977048-1.0.1.1-TOfuUhVA.hcAhp_zyaSQ3eamaCcAB8BbUp19PJBetsM))

  


For the following year, I am seeking funding to continue my research and other activities. Specific initiatives I am carrying out include:

  * [A low-cost service](http://lpsettlement.support/) for helping victims of the LastPass data breach file claims to recoup their losses via a class acton lawsuit.
