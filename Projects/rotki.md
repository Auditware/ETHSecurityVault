## Description

rotki is a fully opensource (AGPL) portfolio tracking, accounting & management app that puts users first. Unlike most financial apps, rotki never extracts your data, never locks you into someone else’s system, and never monetizes your privacy. It’s local-first software: your entire financial history lives on your machine, under your control.

  


In a world where most crypto apps are cloud-based and data-hungry, rotki is different. It’s an **aligned app,** built to serve its users, not exploit them.

  


## Why rotki matters

Crypto is spread across centralized exchanges, multiple chains, and countless protocols. The result: a chaotic mess of wallets, trades, and balances. rotki brings clarity and control:

  


  * **Unified portfolio view** across exchanges, blockchains, and DeFi protocols.
  * **Historical insight** into your entire crypto journey: profits, losses, and positions.
  * **Accounting & reporting tools** that help you share results with an accountant without ever giving up your private data.
  * **Privacy by design** : no trackers, no hidden APIs, no accounts in someone else’s database.



  


## How rotki improves Ethereum security

Every centralized portfolio tracker and tax tool is an attack surface. They collect wallet addresses, exchange API keys, full transaction histories, and net worth data into centralized databases. When these services get breached,and they do as we unfortunately keep seeing, attackers get a ready-made target list: who holds what, where, and how much. This is the reconnaissance phase of phishing, SIM-swap, and wrench attacks, handed over voluntarily by users who just wanted to track their portfolio. Today in France there is a crypto-related kidnapping every 2 days!!!! rotki eliminates this entire class of vulnerability by design:

  


  * No centralized honeypot. User data is encrypted and stored locally. There is no database of high-value wallets, api keys or history to exfiltrate. A breach of rotki's servers cannot compromise user financial data. We don't have them.
  * Open-source verifiability. Unlike closed-source competitors, rotki's codebase is fully auditable. Users and security researchers can verify there is no data exfiltration, no hidden telemetry, and no supply chain risk from opaque dependencies. This is trustless verification applied to financial tooling.
  * Privacy by design. No trackers, no hidden APIs, no accounts in someone else's database.



  


rotki proves that the crypto industry's unfortunately default pattern "give us all your data and trust us" is a choice, not a technical necessity.

  


## Advocating for data sovereignty across Ethereum

  


Beyond the software itself, rotki's founder Lefteris Karapetsas actively uses his platform (~80k followers on X alone) to advocate for data sovereignty, local-first architecture, and privacy-preserving design across the Ethereum ecosystem. This includes raising awareness about the risks of centralized data collection in crypto tooling, the problem of centralized indexer dependencies (where even public blockchain data becomes gated by private companies), and the broader case that Ethereum's security depends not just on protocol hardening but on how users interact with the ecosystem day-to-day. rotki isn't just building an alternative it's making the case, publicly and consistently, that the Ethereum community deserves better than surveillance by default financial tools.

  


## Where we’re going

  


rotki is already the most advanced local-first portfolio manager, but the vision is much bigger:

  * More supported protocols, chains, and integrations.
  * Smarter search, filtering, and analytics.
  * Portfolio management features that let users act, not just observe.
  * Continued innovation around **privacy-preserving, user-aligned financial tools**.



  


## Why support matters

  


Because rotki refuses to monetize users through ads, lock-ins, or surveillance, we rely on **community funding** to keep the project alive. Donations directly support development, maintenance, and new features ensuring rotki remains independent, transparent, and aligned with its users.

  


By supporting rotki, you’re not just funding one app. You’re helping prove that **aligned apps** can survive and thrive: software that empowers people without exploiting them.
