### Who?

We’re the team behind Grego AI, led by 0xriptide. A small group of security researchers, AI engineers, and crypto natives who got tired of watching billions get drained because audits can’t keep up with how complex protocols have become.

  


### What?

Grego AI is a specialized AI system built to autonomously find deep, multi-layer logic flaws in smart contracts and blockchain code that every human auditor misses.

It doesn’t just scan – it maps the entire codebase, traces execution paths, spins up sub-agents, builds real proof-of-concept exploits in a sandbox, iterates, and hands us a clean, reproducible bug report.

  


This is not like other "lightweight" AI scanners as our compute costs are much higher due to the depth of analysis performed by our tool.

  


Last week it found a critical vulnerability that put $28M at risk in a heavily audited protocol and earned the largest bounty an AI has ever received: $250,000 – fully discovered with zero human assistance.

  


### Why?

Because human-only audits have hit their limit.

Codebases are massive. Systems are cross-chain and composable. Even the original devs don’t always see every interaction anymore. Hackers don’t sleep and the blockchain never closes. Billions get lost every year not because teams don’t care, but because the tools we have today aren’t enough.

We built Grego AI to find the bugs humans literally cannot see before the bad guys do.

  


### Where?

Right now we’re focused 100% on Web3 – DeFi protocols, blockchain infrastructure, bridges, DAOs, anything that touches real money on-chain.

We’ve already found live bugs in Lido, Chainlink, Aave, Uniswap, Polygon, ENS, Euler and others.

Long term we want to expand to any critical codebase (Web2 + infrastructure), but crypto is the perfect first battlefield because one missed bug in crypto leads to a massive immediate theft scenario of live funds.

  


### How?

We spent the last two years building this from the ground up.

Not a wrapper, not a basic scanner, but a deeply trained system that thinks like the best security researcher but at a scale and speed no human team can match.
