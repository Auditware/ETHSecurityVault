## 🛡️ What is BlockThreat?

**BlockThreat** (Blockchain Threat Intelligence) is the longest-running independent blockchain security newsletter dedicated to making the DeFi ecosystem safer. For the past **7 years** , BlockThreat has consistently done the work to produce high quality threat intelligence, validate and refine insights, and surface actionable security knowledge for the ecosystem on a **weekly** **basis**.

  


It provides:

  * 🔍 **Weekly breakdowns of security incidents** including root cause analysis, indicators, and actionable insights on how to defend yourself against similar attacks.
  * 🧪 **Latest research & tooling alpha** surfacing cutting-edge techniques to keep defenders and researchers ahead of the curve.
  * 📊 **Continuously updated DeFi Top 10 Attack Vectors** helping defenders prioritize and stay ahead of emerging risks.
  * 📄 **Open-access special reports** including deep dives such as integrating AI into Web3 defense strategies.
