## Description

SEAL 911 is a nonprofit dedicated to safeguarding the blockchain and crypto ecosystem. In an industry plagued with scams, malware, and security flaws, SEAL 911 steps in to provide critical protection for users, developers, and projects in danger. With your support, we can secure more wallets, protect valuable funds, and make crypto a safer space for all.

  


We founded SEAL 911 in response to the increasing need for organized security in the crypto space. Since its inception, we've handled over 12,200 [security tickets](https://t.me/seal_911_bot), managing high-stakes incidents like the Curve exploit and countless phishing attempts. Our focus is on collaboration, innovation, and rapid response—ensuring that whenever a security threat arises, the community has a trusted ally to turn to.

  


### Our Impact in 2024

  


The need for SEAL 911 has never been more urgent. In 2024, our team of 28 **passionate volunteers** has achieved outstanding results:

  * **+1,500 tickets handled** to resolve security incidents.
  * **75+ war rooms actively managed** to mitigate ongoing exploits.
  * **150,000+ phishing domains blocked** to protect users.
  * [**Incident Resolution Log**](https://github.com/security-alliance/seal-911/blob/main/ACTIVITY.md)
  * **~$75M USD saved** through phishing prevention, smart contract rescues, and other critical actions.



We’ve tackled some of the most dangerous threats in the crypto space, including:

  1. Phishing attacks
  2. Private key leaks
  3. Malware infections
  4. Social media account hijacks
  5. Smart contract hacks
  6. Pig Butchering scams (Sha Zhu Pan)
  7. Vulnerability disclosures
  8. Phishing URL reports
  9. Frontrunning and wallet rescues by white hats
  10. Domain hijacks



  


Despite these successes, the road ahead is long. The security landscape in crypto still lags behind where it needs to be, and SEAL 911 is here to fill that gap.

  


### How SEAL 911 Works

  


  * **Emergency Response:** Through the SEAL 911 Telegram bot, we offer instant support during security crises.
  * **White Hat Support:** We work closely with [white hats](https://github.com/security-alliance/seal-911#members) to rescue funds, disclose vulnerabilities, and secure protocols.
  * **Proactive Measures:**  We’re constantly blocking phishing domains and developing best practices for war rooms, building a safer crypto environment.



  


### Why We Need Your Help

  


Every SEAL 911 [member](https://github.com/security-alliance/seal-911#members) is a volunteer, and while we work tirelessly with limited resources, the demand for our expertise keeps growing. To expand and continue our efforts, we need your help. Your support will allow us to:

  * Scale up our phishing prevention systems.
  * Build better tools to detect and respond to threats.
  * Provide financial support to volunteers and white hats.
  * Educate and raise awareness on how to avoid risks.



  


By donating to SEAL 911, you're investing in the future of crypto security. Together, we can protect users, projects, and protocols from the dangers that threaten our industry.
