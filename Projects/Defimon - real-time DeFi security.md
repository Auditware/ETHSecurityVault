## Description

In DeFi, the window between an exploit landing onchain and a protocol team being alerted can be critical. Defimon exists to close that window with instant security alerts and automated incident response. Built by the security team at Decurity, it monitors EVM chains 24/7 and fires alerts to the protocol teams, security companies and risk curators before hacks make the news.

  


Defimon is not only a real-time DeFi threat intelligence feed, it's also a whitehat rescue bot that has helped to prevent numerous hacks and directly rescued more than $2 million from the vulnerable contracts. In the age of AI the number of hacks is growing each day. Until the new equilibrium reached, AI should be fought with AI - that's how Defimon's rescue bot manages to intervene in the ongoing incidents before malicious actors could react.
