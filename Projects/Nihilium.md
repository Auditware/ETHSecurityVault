## Description

**Building the "Forgot My Password" Button for Web3**

Imagine never losing access to your crypto wallet, encrypted files, or private data again, without trusting a custodian. That’s the gap Nihilium closes. For years Web3 has delivered self-custody for _authorization_ , but recovery stayed stuck in the seed-phrase-in-a-drawer era.

  


Nihilium delivers the missing middle path: **slashable conditional key release** — a crypto-economic protocol that binds secrets to any NP-style condition (time-locks, ZKEmail, ZKPassport, signatures, oracles, etc.) so only the rightful owner can unseal them.

  


No one ever holds the private key in plaintext. Processors validate client-generated ZK proofs and decrypt only on valid conditions — or lose their entire staked reputation permanently. The result? Self-sovereign recovery that’s censorship-resistant, observable, and actually usable.

  


**Why This Matters**

Encrypted-by-default apps, private transactions, E2EE messaging, and medical-data “break-glass” access all become practical when recovery is reliable. We make the next billion users comfortable with true self-custody.
