## Description

DD.xyz by Webacy is a free, public-good platform that gives anyone the ability to assess risk across wallets, tokens, smart contracts, and transactions in real time. In an ecosystem where billions are lost to scams, exploits, and hidden vulnerabilities, DD brings institutional-grade onchain intelligence to everyday users, developers, and communities.

  


Through a simple interface, users can instantly surface threats like malicious contracts, wallet exposure to hacks or sanctions, token manipulation risks, and transaction-level dangers before it causes more harm. Our mission is to make crypto safer. By funding Webacy DD, you’re supporting open access to critical safety infrastructure that protects users, improves trust in decentralized systems, and raises the standard for security across the entire ecosystem.

  


**Why it matters:**

Crypto moves fast, but safety infrastructure has lagged behind. Webacy DD closes that gap by turning complex onchain data into clear, actionable risk insights anyone can use.

  


**Use of funds:**

Donations will help us maintain and expand free access to DD, improve detection models, support new chains and assets, and continue building public-good tools that keep users safe onchain.

  


**Impact:**

  * Protect users from scams, exploits, and financial loss
  * Increase transparency and trust in DeFi and digital assets
  * Empower builders to integrate safety directly into their products
  * Contribute to a more resilient and secure crypto ecosystem
