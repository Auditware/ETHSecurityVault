## Description

I am a one man army, focusing on anti scam in the web3/cryptocurrency space.

  


Apart from blocking scams and c2 infrastructure i also work on detecting patterns, and API integrations with service providers to secure faster enforcement times.

  


  


My goal is to insert friction to threat actor campaigns on the ZERO minute mark they get spawned.

No matter if it is fake zoom or google meet scam, which deploys a RAT, permit2, drainer, pig butchering, investment scam, exchange with no liquidity or another nonsense such as seed phrase stealer, fake app or fraudulent impersonation social media profiles - i do it all.

Modus operandi

  


I run daily checks against various sources like certstream, newly registered domains, also maintain a database of known bad email addresses, and basically with my daily checks i make sure any missed URLs which are attacks get blocked in a very short time window next to their registration.

  


Each new scam zoom domain which ends on .US i find - save the email and check for the same registrant the next day.

  


Actors often abuse the same infrastructure and or emails, which can be used as leverage against them,

  


  


  


I also mirror the scam data on UrlScan, submit it to various entities like Google safe Browsing, Microsoft Safe Screen, SpamHaus and others to add maximum friction to threat actors.

  


  


I maintain a database of known bad emails, known bad IPs, ASNs, which i re-check daily.

  


Even for cases where domain is in TRANCO - i still have secret sauce up my sleeve.

  


  


I also leave comments on [VirusTotal](https://www.virustotal.com/gui/user/spamreports) so other researchers can also benefit from my input.
