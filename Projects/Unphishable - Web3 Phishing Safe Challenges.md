## Description

Unphishable is a hands-on, interactive platform designed to help users recognize and defend against phishing attacks in the Web3 space. Through realistic, gamified challenges, participants develop practical awareness and strengthen their phishing detection skills.

**The platform features:**

  * 30+ interactive phishing challenges across different difficulty levels (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
  * Multilingual support (English, Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese)
  * Gamified Anti-Phishing Training
  * Learn by doing
  * Real-World Phishing Simulations - Train to recognize and defend against common Web3 attack vectors, including:
  * Seed phrase scams
  * Token approval phishing
  * Airdrop scams
  * Clipboard hijacking
  * Punycode domain attacks
  * And many more advanced attack vectors



**Project Content & Features**

  * **Phishing Technique Simulation:** Simulate common Web3 phishing attack scenarios, such as fake wallet websites, fake airdrop scams, fraudulent contract signing, and fake transaction confirmations.
  * Provide an interactive simulation environment where users can experience phishing attacks firsthand and recognize their characteristics.
  * **Education & Training ModulesCase Analysis:** Offer detailed breakdowns of historical phishing cases, including attack techniques, characteristics, and prevention measures.
  * **Scenario-Based Exercises:** Develop a practice question bank to test users' phishing identification skills, progressing from basic to advanced levels.
  * **Prevention Techniques Training:** Teach users how to identify phishing websites, verify transaction security, and implement multi-layer protection (e.g., hardware wallets, multi-signature authentication).
  * **Community Contribution & Collaboration**Invite security researchers and developers to contribute new phishing cases and defense techniques.
  * Establish discussion groups to encourage experience sharing and technical exchanges among users.
  * **Learning Progress Tracking** Provide users with phishing detection score assessments and personalized learning recommendations.
  * Offer exclusive certificates for completing phishing prevention courses to enhance users' credibility and security awareness. **(Future Development)**
  * **Multi-Language Support & User-Friendly Design**Support multiple languages (including Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese, and English) to ensure global accessibility.
  * Implement a clean UI design and an intuitive user experience to lower the learning barrier.
