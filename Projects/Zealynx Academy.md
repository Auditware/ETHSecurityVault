## Description

Zealynx Academy is a free, interactive platform to help Web3 founders build secure protocols and grow them into real businesses. No videos, no slides. You write every function yourself, guided by starter code and automated test suites. Visit the platform: <https://academy.zealynx.io>

  


Ethereum's security depends on a growing pipeline of skilled builders and auditors. Right now, most security education is either passive (watch and read) or locked behind expensive bootcamps. Zealynx Academy makes this education accessible to everyone, completely **FREE** , with no paywall and no premium tier.

  


Four things inside:

  


\- Guides to fork DeFi protocols with awareness

\- Security training to build safer Smart Contracts

\- MBA-level knowledge to grow your protocol into a business

\- Interactive guides to build your own AI Agents to help you grow your company

  


**Build it before you fork it.**

  


You rebuild the protocol from scratch. Guided. Line by line. Until your code matches the original.

  


Not to learn Solidity. To actually understand what you're about to fork. What you have, what you want to keep, what you want to change, what you want to throw out.

  


Our first module rebuilds Uniswap V2 from zero. 8 sections. 207 automated tests. You don't finish until every test passes. You can also find Compound V2 already, with more coming.

  


**The security training.**

  


You shadow-audit a past contest of a fork of what you just built.

  


Basin. ElasticSwap. Velodrome. Flux Finance. Canto v2. Five real protocol forks, 10,163 lines of Solidity, 46 documented bugs.

  


You see how other builders forked. What they kept. What they broke. What the attack surface looks like on the thing you're about to ship a version of.

  


When the time-boxed shadow audit ends, you get the full review and learnings. Security mindset built into the foundation, before you ever deploy.

  


**The eMBA for Web3 Founders.**

  


Tokenomics. Fundraising. Governance. Treasury. Regulatory. Go-to-market.

  


Because most of us are technical, and launching a protocol is much harder than we think we know. The eMBA handles everything we don't know we don't know.

  


**Create your own AI Auditor Agent.**

  


An interactive guide with multiple paths, each modeled after the most powerful existing AI auditor tools. Pick your path. Build your agent. Then benchmark it in the AI Auditor Arena against 118 real Code4rena bugs across 10 contests.

  


If you're shipping AI audit tools, this is where you find out if yours actually works.

  


**The whole platform is gamified by design.**

  


7 ranks from newcomer to sovereign. A public leaderboard. Lynx and badges earned through real work.

  


Because in Web3, _your reputation is your resume_. Zealynx Academy turns every lesson, every bug you find, every module you finish into a verifiable public track record.

  


Zealynx

  


The platform is built by [Zealynx Security](zealynx.io), a boutique smart contract audit firm with 40+ protocol audits under its belt. Community feedback and collaboration happen through our public GitHub repository (<https://github.com/ZealynxSecurity/zealynx-academy-feedback)> and the Academy Insiders Telegram group.

  


The platform code is not open source to protect educational integrity. Test solutions, challenge scaffolding, and Shadow Arena finding catalogs would lose their educational value if publicly available. This follows the same model used by Cyfrin Updraft, RareSkills, and Node Guardians.

  


**How Funding Scales Our Impact**

  


Every dollar goes toward people and content, not infrastructure markup. The platform stays free for all users regardless of funding level.

  


Under $5k: We cover infrastructure costs out of pocket and continue building modules solo.

  


$5k to $10k: We hire a part time security content creator to ship new modules faster. Priority: Lending/Borrowing, Bridge Security, Oracle Integration, Vault/Staking, and AI/MCP security, each built around real Ethereum exploits with actual vulnerable code.

  


$10k to $25k: We hire a part time developer to accelerate platform features (Exploit Archive, Krait AI integration for automated feedback on student code). We pay working auditors and protocol builders as guest tutors for free weekly live sessions covering building, auditing, and the business side of Web3 security.

  


$25k to $50k: We connect Academy directly with our [Audit Grants program](grants.zealynx.io) (grants.zealynx.io). We fund junior auditor stipends so top graduates can work on real Ethereum protocol audits, mentored by our senior team. We hire a content lead and fund Spanish and Portuguese translations.

  


$50k+: We build a dedicated team (developers, domain experts, marketing) and launch a paid Security Apprenticeship Pipeline: 3 month funded positions where graduates audit real Ethereum protocols alongside professionals. Goal: 20+ job ready security researchers per year feeding into the Ethereum ecosystem.
