## Description

**Cyfrin Updraft — Free Web3 Education for Everyone**

  


**Who?** Cyfrin Updraft is the #1 smart contract developer and security researcher education platform on earth, built by Cyfrin — a leading blockchain security firm. Our graduates work at Coinbase, MetaMask, OpenZeppelin, Chainlink, Aave, and more.

  


**What?** We provide 100+ hours of free, hands-on courses covering everything from Blockchain Basics to Advanced Foundry, Smart Contract Security, Formal Verification, and DeFi protocol deep dives. Over 200,000 students taught, 60,000+ monthly active learners, and 1,000,000+ lessons watched.

  


**Why?** Web3 is only as strong as its builders. Every developer priced out of education is a protocol that never gets built — or worse, one that gets hacked. Free education means more secure protocols and a healthier ecosystem for everyone.

  


**Where?** Fully online and asynchronous at updraft.cyfrin.io. Learn from anywhere, at your own pace.

  


**How?** Donations keep courses free: funding new content, compensating world-class instructors, maintaining the platform, and developing hands-on projects and industry-recognized certifications.

  


Help train the next generation of developers and security researchers protecting the protocols we all depend on.
