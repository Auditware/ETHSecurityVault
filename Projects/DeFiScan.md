## Description

**DeFiScan** is a research [framework](https://www.defiscan.info/learn-more) and analytics [dashboard](https://www.defiscan.info/) offering comprehensive and unbiased insights into the decentralisation level of EVM DeFi applications. DeFiScan categorizes projects according to their decentralization stages and single-point-of-failure vulnerabilities, providing verifiable insights into DeFi protocol maturity and risks.

DeFiScan promotes transparency, education, self-policing, and informed decision-making in DeFi.

  


DeFiScan has been created by **The DeFi Collective** , a [non-profit association](https://deficollective.org/about/) whose goal is to promote genuinely decentralised DeFi protocols and is behind a whole range of initiatives to support those protocols:

  * The Collective helps protocols to bootstrap liquidity for the hardest assets. It has already secured donations for liquidity-driving positions to achieve this goal and is actively seeking additional ones.
  * The decentralisation of public infrastructure is an active field of research, and understanding the drivers of security, reliability, and efficiency is crucial to protecting users and their funds. We conduct and support research that furthers the understanding of this field.
  * Developing and promoting a self-policing framework for DeFi protocols, such as **DeFiScan.**



  


**DeFiScan is our flagship project.**

**We already covered more than 50% TVL of the entire DeFi landscape, failing to reach our 90% by the end of 2025.**

This is due to the fact that **we are actively working on a second version of our framework** that enables live monitoring of TVL at risks, and bring more granularity into centralization risks.

  


**Our goal is to reach 90% TVL coverage by Q3 2026, and to update DeFiScan’s framework by Q2.**
