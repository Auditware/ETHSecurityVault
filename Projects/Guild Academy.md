## Description

# Guild Academy is a public-goods Web3 security training program.

## About Us

Guild Academy trains developers, cybersecurity professionals, and blockchain builders into capable smart contract security researchers through a 16-week intensive bootcamp that runs twice a year — combining deep technical education with real-world auditing practice.

What makes us different is our **dedicated physical training facility** with stable power, internet, devices, and accommodation — removing the infrastructure barriers that prevent talented individuals in emerging regions from accessing high-quality security education. We also run a full online track, reaching participants across Africa, Asia, Europe, and the Americas.

  


We are not a course platform. We are a security talent pipeline — training people to think adversarially, break real systems, and ship verifiable output.

  


## Our Impact (5 Cohorts)

**Talent** : 300+ security researchers trained across 5 cohorts spanning multiple continents. 48 researchers in the most recent cohort alone. 221 applications from 22 countries received for a single cohort cycle.

  


**Vulnerabilities** : 200+ vulnerabilities reported (High, Medium, Low) across Code4rena, Sherlock, Codehawks, Cantina, and Immunefi. Several alumni ranked in the top 10 on global audit leaderboards. 25+ competitive audits participated in.

  


**Research** : 55+ technical articles published. 40+ real-world hacks replayed and analyzed. Student-led EIPs proposed. AI-assisted security tools built by students (Blocksploit, Solvra).

  


**Ecosystem** : Pro-bono audits delivered for open-source projects including Coinsafe, SIR Protocol, Fractal Visions, Paynode, and PayCrypt. Open-source hack labs and training repos maintained on GitHub. StarkNet Cairo security bootcamp partnership. Formal Immunefi training partner.

  


**Recognition** : Selected as one of 17 recipients of the **Ethereum Foundation's ETH Rangers Program** — a public-goods security stipend alongside Secureum, The Red Guild, and the Security Alliance (SEAL). Founder recognized as **Ethereum Foundation Next Billion Fellow**.

  


## Student-Built Security Tools

One of our most impactful outcomes has been students building their own security tooling:

**Blocksploit** — an AI-powered smart contract analysis tool for automated vulnerability detection and code behavior explanation.

**Solvra** — a security research assistant that combines static analysis with AI-driven pattern recognition to accelerate audit workflows.

These tools demonstrate that our graduates are not just consumers of security methodology — they are builders of next-generation security infrastructure.

  


## Community & Ecosystem Building

We organized the **Web3 Security Summit Africa** — a landmark event held in Lagos on November 8, 2025, bringing together security researchers, developers, auditors, and ecosystem leaders for workshops, technical presentations, and panel discussions on smart contract vulnerabilities and the future of decentralized security. It was the first event of its kind on the continent, and we are committed to making it a recurring institution.

Our alumni are active across major audit platforms — ranking on leaderboards, contributing to private audits and public contests, earning from bug bounties, building security tools, and returning to mentor new cohorts. Each cohort strengthens the next.

  


## Why Support Matters Now

Web3 continues to grow, but security talent remains critically limited. Exploits cost the ecosystem billions, often due to preventable vulnerabilities.

Guild Academy addresses this at the root: by training people who can identify, understand, and prevent these vulnerabilities before they become exploits.

Support at this stage is not about starting something new. It is about **scaling what is already working** : training more researchers, expanding physical infrastructure and tooling, supporting participants who otherwise would not have access, increasing open-source security contributions, and growing the Web3 Security Summit Africa into a permanent institution.

# We are building critical security infrastructure through people. And we intend to keep going.
