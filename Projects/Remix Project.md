## Description

**Remix...**

the world’s most widely used online [Web3 development environment](https://remix.live/), is maintained to ensure the core of decentralized technology can remain an essential public good. By stewarding Web3 education and advocacy, [Remix](https://remix.live/) bridges the gap between frontier technology and the builders solving 21st-century challenges in P2P finance, AI safety, circular economies, sustainable development, and sovereign identity/data in health care, governance, and physical infrastructure.

  


We are entering a new era with Remix v2 and our Model Context Protocol (MCP) which now integrates Web3 infra endpoints like OpenZeppelin, Etherscan, The Graph, ENS, and L2s with premier LLMs, Ethereum Skills modules enhancing security, and sector-specific Skills outside Web3. This technological leap enables humans and AI to co-create complex, secure products from zero to dApp with unprecedented speed inside Remix.

  


**Web3 AI Safety**

To support Ethereum L1/L2 security over the next 12 months, Remix seeks $100,000 to evolve the IDE into a proactive security gateway that bridges the gap between advanced Ethereum defensive tooling and the sector-specific builder/entrepeneur. Our primary focus will be the orchestration of AI sub-agents and the integration of emerging standards like x402. We intend to build a framework that coordinates multiple specialized AI agents to perform cross-referenced contract analysis, formal verification, and scam mitigation. By surfacing expert-level security protocols and tooling through our MCP, Remix endpoints will serve as an effective Web3 safety guardrail. 

  


This initiative also includes implementing human-in-the-loop AI safety measures to prevent hallucinations, and provide pragmatic security education to ensure that newcomers have the same access to cutting-edge security "skills" as the most advanced researchers in the ecosystem.
