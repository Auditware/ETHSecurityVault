## Description

Aragon powers protocols with products and services designed to manage capital allocation, governance, and ownership onchain.

  


# Founded in 2017, built**  to endure the evolution of crypto.**

  


Aragon began laying the foundations for organizations to exist, function, and thrive onchain.

  


Eight years later, our infrastructure secures over $35B across networks like Lido, Polygon, Taiko, Morpho, and Curve, with zero security incidents since inception. From the original AragonOS to the modular Aragon OSx, we’ve evolved alongside the ecosystem, keeping flexibility, security, and openness at our core.

  


Since day one, Aragon has worked to make running your project onchain practical, secure, and scalable. We believe that protocols should be able to evolve without friction or tradeoffs, upgrading their logic, adjusting incentives, and distributing ownership as they grow.

  


We’re a distributed team of over 25 specialists working across smart contract engineering, protocol design, and policy. Our jurisdiction is Switzerland as the 2nd Ethereum entity in Switzerland after the Ethereum Foundation itself. Aragon launched the 2nd Ethreum grants program in history helping fund projects like Prysm, Espresso, DappNode, and Snapshot. The organisation has also breached into web2 after having purchased Vocdoni (now an entity on its own once again), bringing blockchain based voting to organisations and municipalities across Spain.

  


Our focus is long-term and onchain: building tools and standards that enable resilient, secure, and composable systems for the next generation of networks.
