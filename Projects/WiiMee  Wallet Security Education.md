## Description

Users need to feel safe to act confidently in crypto.

And still, every day, users lose funds not because of sophisticated smart contract exploits but because of simple, preventable mistakes. Signing malicious transactions, exposing seed phrases, interacting with compromised websites or falling for social engineering attacks.

  


The education gap is massive, and I'm here to help make wallet security more understandable and actionable for everyday users. Since 2021 I've been focused on closing the gap with content in written and video form around security education.

  


The content is designed to meet users where they are, on social platforms like X and YouTube.

My goal is to simplify complex security concepts into clear insights. The variety of topics goes from wallet hygiene to approval risks, seed phrase security, malware threats, smart account risks (EIP-7702) to emerging scam patterns.

  


The end goal is to prevent losses before they happen. Because this is really the biggest weapon we have. I hate to be the guy to break it down to a person that got scammed, that they will never see their funds again.

  


I'm deeply invested in the security ecosystem and I'm currently working with Revoke.cash, Boring Security and ChainPatrol to elevate and distribute education even further.
