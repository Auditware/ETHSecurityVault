## Description

L2BEAT is a public goods company dedicated to providing on-chain transparency.

  


What sets L2BEAT apart is our unwavering commitment to delivering accurate and reliable information. We evaluate projects based on our core principles of censorship resistance, open source, privacy and security.

  


This approach has earned us recognition and a solid reputation in the industry, garnering praise from the global Ethereum community and making our voice highly valued by numerous organizations and institutions. We are unafraid of being vocal and opinionated when our values are at stake.

  


Our work spans a wide range of areas, including:

  * Research and analysis of public protocols, including L2s and interop
  * Monitoring of smart contract upgrades
  * Participation in protocol governance and security councils



  


All of our work is publicly accessible on our GitHub Repo - <https://github.com/l2beat/l2beat>

We announce all new features, events, and other endeavors on our Twitter account - <https://twitter.com/l2beat>

  


Your donations are the fuel to our mission, bolstering our relentless endeavors to usher in education, clarity, and invaluable tools to the L2 universe.

  


Your unwavering support means the world to us. Thank you! 💗
