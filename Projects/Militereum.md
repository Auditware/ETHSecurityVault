## Description

**Militereum is a web3 firewall for your crypto wallets.**

[Militereum](https://militereum.com) simulates your transactions and shows you exactly what tokens are about to exit your wallet BEFORE a transaction leaves your desktop. And if Militereum thinks your transaction is unsafe, it is blocked.

  


**What chains and wallets does Militereum support?**

Militereum is chain-agnostic (supporting L2s such as [Optimism](https://optimism.io/) and [Arbitrum](https://arbitrum.io/)) and works with every EVM-compatible crypto wallet, including browser-based and native desktop wallets.

  


**What are the goals of this project?**

This project is a success if our users are prevented from getting phished/drained/rugged at least once.

  


**How will you use the funds?**

We will use the funds to improve the product and add (both static and AI-driven) analysis of your transactions for more and more known attack vectors.

  


**Features**

  * simulates your transactions
  * shows you exactly what tokens are about to exit your wallet BEFORE a transaction leaves your desktop
  * blocks suspicious transactions
  * works with every EVM-compatible wallet, including browser-based (eg. [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/), [Trust](https://trustwallet.com/)) and native desktop (eg. [Frame](https://frame.sh/), [Zerion](https://link.zerion.io/a11o6IN0jqb)) wallets
  * non-custodial, 100% uptime. you do not depend on our servers because there aren't any
  * supports Ethereum and many other chains, including [Polygon](https://polygon.technology/) and [Arbitrum](https://arbitrum.io/) and [Optimism](https://optimism.io/) and [Base](https://base.org/)
  * available for [Windows](https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9nv0c48z57r4) and [macOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/militereum/id6446287995)



  


Last but not least, Militereum is free and [open source](https://github.com/Militereum/Militereum#readme). There are no VCs and there is no token.
